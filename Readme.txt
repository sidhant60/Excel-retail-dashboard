📊 Retail Sales Dashboard (2022–2024)
This Excel dashboard visualizes and analyzes retail sales data across various categories and regions from 2022 to 2024.

🔍 Features:
Total sales summary with key metrics

Sales breakdown by product category

Regional sales comparison

Price comparison with competitors

Average stock surplus by category

Interactive slicers for year and weather- wise filtering

🧰 Tools Used:
Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts, Slicers)

Basic Data Cleaning & Transformation

🎯 Purpose:
Designed as a portfolio project to demonstrate Excel dashboarding skills relevant for data analysis and reporting roles.
